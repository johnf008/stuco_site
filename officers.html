Officers

Officer positions will be held by students that are voted on by their class or the council to hold office for either their class or executive. Class officer positions will be President, Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer. There will be one for each class (9th, 10th, 11th and 12th). For executive positions, they are the same as class except they have other positions such as Historians. The executive board will also be students that will be in charge of STUCO as a whole, not only their respective class.
